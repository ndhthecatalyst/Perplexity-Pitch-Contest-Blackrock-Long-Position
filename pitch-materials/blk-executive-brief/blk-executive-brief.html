Investment Thesis

01
What BlackRock Is

BlackRock is the only asset manager that is simultaneously the world's largest ETF provider — anchoring $5.5T in iShares AUM with approximately 40% of global ETF market share — a rapidly scaling private markets platform with $423.6B in alternatives AUM, and the operator of Aladdin, the dominant institutional risk management and portfolio operating system overseeing $21.6T across more than 200 client institutions globally. No single competitor has assembled all three capabilities at this scale.

02
Why Now

The combination of the GIP, HPS, and Preqin acquisitions has fundamentally changed BlackRock's earnings quality mix — from AUM-sensitive base fees toward recurring technology subscriptions and higher-margin private markets revenue. Technology services grew 24% year-over-year in Q4 2025; alternatives fees grew 136% through the first three quarters of 2025. This revenue mix transformation, now approaching 20% of total, is not yet reflected in a stock trading at approximately 22x forward earnings.
